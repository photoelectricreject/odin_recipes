Oven baked chicken

Description

Follow these steps and get some delicious chicken.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breasts
  • Seasonings
  • Rice

Steps

  1. Put cold water in bowl. Soak chicken in water for at least 15 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 400 degrees.
  3. Add chicken to baking tray and season the chicken aggressively
  4. Cook chicken in oven for about 20 minutes.
