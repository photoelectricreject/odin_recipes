Veggie lentil soup

Description

This a wonderful recipe for lentil soup with vegetables. This dish takes about 45 minutes to cook.

Ingredients

  • An onion and some garlic
  • Lentils
  • Some vegetables like carrots or asparagus or celery
  • Vegetable broth
  • Diced tomatoes

Steps

  1. Chop onions, garlic, and veggies.
  2. Add some olive oil to pot set to medium heat and add onions, garlic, and veggies. Cook until onions turn color.
  3. Add diced tomatoes, lentils, and broth to pot. Cover the pot.
  4. Once the pot starts to boil, turn down heat. Cook for about 20-30 more minutes.
