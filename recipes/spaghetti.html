Spaghetti

Description

This is a simple recipe for one pot spaghetti with meat sauce. You'll take onions, garlic, spaghetti, meath broth, and meatballs and cook them all in one pot for about 15 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Dried spaghetti
  • One lb ground beef
  • 8-16 oz meat broth
  • One can crushed tomatoes
  • One cup onions
  • Some garlic
  • Some chopped parsely

Steps

  1. Break up beef into small balls and put in bowl.
  2. Season meatballs.
  3. Add some olive oil to pot that's set to medium heat.
  4. Chop onions and garlic and put in pot.
  5. Add meatballs to pot and stir until cooked.
  6. Add crushed tomatoes, meat broth, and chopped parsely to pot and stir.
  7. Add dried spaghetti and cover the pot. Cook for about 15 minutes (maybe stir after 10 minutes).
